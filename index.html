VG-VRT (Video-Game Vestibular Rehabilitation Therapy)

  • Worked on a two-person team to build a Unity application that simulates traditional exercises for vertigo treatment in an augmented reality (AR) environment.
  • The player follows a ball's motion back and forth with their head. The player's performance is evaluated based on how closely their gaze matched the position of the ball. Each time the exercise is performed, the ball and gaze positions over time are logged in a CSV file for researcher use.
